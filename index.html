Student Self-Service Portal

Given the rise in cybercrime, and before making any payment to the University, verify that the account into which payment is to be made is a legitimate bank account of the University, as published in the Fee Booklet or on the Fee Statement. The University will not communicate any change in bank details to students by way of email, bank confirmation letters or other electronic communication. Please check that the University’s bank details are accurate before making payment. It is your duty as the Payee to check that the University’s bank details are accurate before making payment.

This portal works best with latest versions of Chrome and Firefox.

What can I do as an Applicant?

  • Upload your supporting documents.
  • View Academic Applications and their statuses.
  • View your Financial Aid Application.
  • View your Residence Status.
  • View your Personal Information.

What can I do as a Student?

  • Access and print your Fee Statement.
  • Check your timetable and venues.
  • View and amend Personal Information.
  • Check your application status.
  • Pay your fees online.
  • View final examination results.
  • View examination timetable.
  • View graduation dates.

Note

  • If any of the information is incorrect or if you have any queries, please consult your faculty office.
  • If you have any queries please contact the Student Call Centre on 0117171888.
